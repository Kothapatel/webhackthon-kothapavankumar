Here are 10 cardio exercises for you to stay healthy !

1.squat Jump

How to do?

  • Start in a squat (feet under shoulders, toes facing forward, thighs parallel to floor) with torso upright and hands clasped in front of chest.
  • SPress through feet to straighten legs and jump up off the floor while swinging straight arms behind body.
  • Start by inhaling, engaging your core, and bending your knees while pushing your butt out.
  • Land back in a squat position.
